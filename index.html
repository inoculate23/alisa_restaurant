About Us

Welcome to Alisa Restaurant, where traditional Lao flavors meet the serene beauty of the Mekong River. Located just steps away from the famous Slow Boat dock in Pakbeng, our restaurant offers an unforgettable dining experience with stunning river views.

Founded by local chef Alisa, our restaurant specializes in authentic Lao dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're a backpacker arriving by boat or a traveler exploring the region, we provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can savor delicious food while watching the gentle flow of the Mekong.

Our menu features traditional favorites like Larb, Tam Mak Hoong, and Ping Gai, alongside unique creations that blend local flavors with international influences. Don't miss our signature cocktails made with local ingredients!
